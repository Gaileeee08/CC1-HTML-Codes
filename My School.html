University of The Cordilleras

A Beacon of Higher Education Beaming from these Majestic Mountain Highlands...

What?

The University of the Cordilleras is a private university in Baguio City, Philippines. The school is in the heart of the city, near Our Lady of Atonement Church, hotels, restaurants, and shopping malls. It has Autonomous status and is an ISO certified university, formerly known as Baguio Colleges Foundation.

Where?

Gov. Pack Rd, Baguio, Benguet

How?

Trimester Program

A trend-setting University with a history of pursuing the innovative path, UC conducts its academic programs on a trimestral basis. A four-year course can be completed in three years, resulting in huge savings in the living expenses of students, together with a fast and focused track to the professional world.
